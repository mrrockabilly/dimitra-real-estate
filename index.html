About

Dimitra is a long time resident of California, where she has lived all her adult life. She attended college in San Joaquin Valley, Long Beach, Pasadena and Carson California. Dimitra has a college degree in Chemistry, and certificates in Supply Chain Management, System Engineering and Real Estate. Dimitra loves California where she lives, works and is raising her family. She has worked for the Texaco Oil Co. in Wilmington CA and for major Aerospace companies in El Segundo CA, including Boeing, where she was an engineer and managed multi-million dollar programs.

Dimitra is very familiar with the neighborhoods, schools, businesses and quality of life in the South Bay and especially Palos Verdes, where she resides. She is a Palos Verdes Real Estate Specialist. She would love to share her knowledge and insight about the area and will work with you to help meet your real estate needs and goals.

Dimitra is proficient in English and Greek.
